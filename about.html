I am a PhD student in the Department of Psychology at The Univeristy of British Columbia, working with Dr. Luke Clark at The Centre for Gambling Research. My research aims to advance our knowledge of human behavior and serve as a cornerstorne to reduce harms associated with digital technology use and inform evidence-based policies.

My research ethos is rooted in passion and openness. I am passionate about the craft and rigour at each stage of the research process, including open science practices. I have made all my articles and data publicy available (here). Also, all my graduate school application materials are shared (PDF) in hopes that it will be helpful for prospective graduate students.

Along with research, I like to play sports, enjoy video games, and explore tasty foods.
