Hi there,
I'm Raymond Wu.

I'm a PhD student in the Cognitive Science stream, an NSERC Doctoral Fellow, and a member of the Centre for Gambling Research at the University of British Columbia, Vancouver.

SOWN SEEDS
I grew up in Toronto (Scarborough district). In my youth, I was not sure if I would make it to, and then through, undergrad, let alone envisioning becoming an "academic" or a "scientist". But, my longing continued to grow and this prospect slowly became more and more conceivable.

DRIVE AND DIRECTION
I am passionate about the craft and rigour at each stage of the research process, from conceptualization to analysis & writing to public outreach. My goal is to advance our knowledge of human behavior, reduce harms associataed with digital technology use, and inform evidence-based policies.

RESEARCH ETHOS
My work is rooted in transparency. This includes open science practices, having made all my articles and data publicy available (here). My graduate school application materials are also shared (PDF) in hopes that it will be helpful for prospective graduate students.

 WORKING TOGETHER
One of my favorite part of work is collaborating with people, especially in environments that foster challenge and creativitiy as well as safety and inclusivity, whether it's for research, teaching, or public outreach. If anything catches your attention, feel free to reach out :)


Along with research, I like to play sports, explore tasty foods, and relax with video games.
