Hi there,
I'm Raymond Wu.

I'm a PhD student in Cognitive Science, an NSERC Doctoral Fellow, and a member of the Centre for Gambling Research at the University of British Columbia, Vancouver.

Drive & Direction 🚀
My goal is to advance our understanding of human behavior, especially in the context of consuming addictive (digital) products, and to inform evidence-based policies.


Research Ethos 💓
My work is rooted in rigour and transparency. This includes open science practices, having made our research and data publicly available, many of which are preregistered.


Working Together 🤝
One of my favorite parts of work is collaborating with other people, especially in environments that foster challenge and creativity as well as safety and inclusion.













Along with research, I enjoy playing sports, exploring tasty foods, and (trying to) relax with video games.
