Hi, I'm Ray!

I'm a cogntive scientist, an NSERC doctoral fellow, and a member of the Centre for Gambling Research at UBC Vancouver.

My research interests are on higher-order cognitions, such as decision-making and emotions, and interactions between people and digital technology. My PhD focuses on modern gambling products.

To this end, I adopt a quantiative perspective and employ a multi-method approach, integrating laboratory experiments with intensive longitudinal surveys.

My goal is to produce work that is rigorous, transparent, and (hopefully) innovative. This includes incoporating open science practices such as preregistration and making data and code publicly available.
